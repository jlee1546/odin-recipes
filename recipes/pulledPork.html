Pulled Pork

Description:

This is a basic recipe for creating pulled pork. While the method is very simple, the result is a smokey and savory delight.

The number of servings or portion sizes is based upon the size of the cut of meat. For more servings, use a larger cut. And for smaller portions, use a smaller cut. Cooking time will vary.

Ingredients

  • Whole Pork Shoulder
  • Season Rub of Your Choice

Steps

  1. Preheat Smoker to 225 degrees F
  2. While smoker is preheating, liberally apply seasoning rub to entire pork shoulder.
  3. Place pork shoulder into smoker, insert temperature probe, and close the smoker lid.
  4. Wait...
  5. Wait...
  6. Once pork shoulder has obtained proper temperature
  7. Allow meat to rest for up to 20 minutes before pulling and enjoying
