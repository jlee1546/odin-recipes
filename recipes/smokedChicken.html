Smoked Chicken

Description:

This is a basic recipe for creating a smoked chicken. While the method is very simple, the result is a smokey and savory delight.

The number of servings or portion sizes is based upon the size of the whole chickens used. For more servings, use larger birds. And for smaller portions, use smaller birds. Cooking time will vary.

Ingredients

  • Whole Chickens
  • Season Rub of Your Choice

Steps

  1. Preheat Smoker to 225 degrees F
  2. While smoker is preheating, liberally apply seasoning rub to whole chickens.
  3. Place whole chickens into smoker, insert temperature probe, and close the smoker lid.
  4. Wait...
  5. Wait...
  6. Once whole chickens have obtained proper temperature
  7. Allow chickens to rest for up to 20 minutes before enjoying.
